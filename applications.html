More advantages, new possibilities

The ELERIA system solves the problems of the traditional BACKLIT system and thus finds new constructive opportunities.

HOMOGENEOUS LIGHT

Thanks to its laser striping system, it distributes the light evenly.

SPACE-SAVING

LED panel is only 9 mm thick, compared to the 8-15 cm of the traditional system or LED drawer.

SECURITY

System pre-installed, tested and sealed to prevent assembly damage or component failure.

EASY INSTALLATION

Through its patented male/female pin system between panels.

CUSTOM MADE

Panel sizes are 100% customizable for small or large format.

COST SAVINGS

As it is a prefabricated system, only one installer is needed to install it on site. Avoiding expenses in other specialists.

EASY REPAIR

Reversible system that allows easy repair of any light failure.
